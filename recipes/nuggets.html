Chicken Nuggets

Panko-breaded chicken nuggets

Ingredients

  • Panko breading
  • Chicken-meat mush

Steps

  1. Throw nuggets in air fryer.
  2. Cook for 9 minutes at 360 degrees F.
  3. Flip nuggets and cook an additional 9 minutes.
  4. Put on plate and enjoy!
