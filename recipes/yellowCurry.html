Yellow Curry

Yellow curry with chicken and white rice

Ingredients

  • Package of Kevin's yellow chicken curry
  • White rice

Steps

  1. Heat rice and water in microwave for five minutes.
  2. Heat up chicken in pan for 1-2 minutes per side.
  3. Pour curry onto chicken and heat for 30 seconds.
  4. Fluff rice with fork and pour curry over rice.
  5. Enjoy!
