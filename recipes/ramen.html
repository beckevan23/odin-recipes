Chicken Ramen Noodles

Chicken flavored instant ramen noodles

Ingredients

  • Enriched flour
  • Palm oil
  • Salt
  • Various seasonings

Steps

  1. Bring two cups water to boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium.
  3. Add noodles and a quarter of the seasoning packet.
  4. Cook for three minutes.
  5. Drain a quarter of the water and add rest of seasoning packet.
  6. Pour in bowl and enjoy!
